Meet your instructor, Jon David "JD" Person, a seasoned trumpeter with 13 years of musical expertise hailing from Little Rock, AR. JD proudly marched with the Crossmen Drum & Bugle Corps from San Antonio, TX during the summers of 2017 and 2018, showcasing his talent both years in Drum Corps International's World Class Finals. JD's musical prowess extends beyond marching, earning him the prestigious title of a double All-State qualifier in 2017 (Choir + Jazz). In the following year, he made Arkansas history by achieving a rare triple All-State qualification (Choir + Concert + Jazz), securing an outstanding 1st Band, 1st Chair placement in the 2018 All-State Jazz Band. In addition to these accomplishments, JD maintained an active role in the Arkansas Symphony Youth Orchestra from 2016 to 2018 under the esteemed direction of Geoffrey Robson, serving as one of his three trumpeters. Embark on a musical journey with JD, where passion meets expertise, and let's elevate your trumpet skills together!
